# 📦 Deliverables - Zam Zam Mandi 3D Animations

## ✅ Completed Components

### 1. FloatingSpices3D.jsx
- **Purpose**: Interactive 3D floating spice particles
- **Location**: `src/components/FloatingSpices3D.jsx`
- **Features**:
  - 6 different spice particles with unique colors
  - Auto-rotating camera with OrbitControls
  - Smooth floating animations
  - Metallic material with emissive glow
  - Perfect for heritage/tradition sections

### 2. Card3DTilt.jsx
- **Purpose**: Mouse-driven 3D tilting cards with shine effects
- **Location**: `src/components/Card3DTilt.jsx`
- **Features**:
  - Real-time mouse tracking
  - 3D perspective rotation
  - Dynamic shine effect
  - Spring physics for smooth animations
  - Customizable styling
  - Perfect for menu items and product cards

### 3. RotatingPlate3D.jsx
- **Purpose**: Beautiful 3D rotating plate with metallic finish
- **Location**: `src/components/RotatingPlate3D.jsx`
- **Features**:
  - Continuously rotating plate
  - Decorative rim and inner rings
  - Metallic materials with realistic lighting
  - Multiple light sources for depth
  - Perfect for hero sections

### 4. Text3D.jsx
- **Purpose**: 3D rotating text with glowing effects
- **Location**: `src/components/Text3D.jsx`
- **Features**:
  - 3D text rendering
  - Auto-rotating animation
  - Floating vertical motion
  - Dual-color glow effect
  - Perfect for headlines and titles

### 5. ParallaxScroll.jsx
- **Purpose**: Scroll-based parallax animations
- **Location**: `src/components/ParallaxScroll.jsx`
- **Features**:
  - Scroll-driven animations
  - Opacity fade in/out
  - Scale transformations
  - Smooth easing
  - Perfect for section transitions
